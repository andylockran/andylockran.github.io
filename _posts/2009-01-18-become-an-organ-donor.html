A few years back, my diabetic aunt had her life changed by a double transplant of Pancreas and Kidney. This was only possible thanks to the kindness and premeditated actions of some kind person to sign on to the organ donor register.

When you die, you don't leave behind a body for people to remember - you leave behind a legacy. The body is no longer of use to you.

I urge those of you who've not yet done so to sign the register - and become an organ donor. In the cold light of day, your organs may be used to save more than one life.. what a fantastic dying wish.
