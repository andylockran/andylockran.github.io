I drive along the urban roads,

The bustle of town mixed with Radio 2.

I see people walking around; enjoying the day.

Some are working - busy under the blue sky.

An angry driver beeps me, ignoring the red light.

Disgraceful.. I have to abide by the law

I think.. do i?

Should I speed, can I

Get away with it?

Reminded of the consequences of my actions and looking back

Ashamed that I too would speed, the very act that hurt me

Close.. I came more than close to it. Yet

Even I would do it
