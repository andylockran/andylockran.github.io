Bit of a long-winded title to a post, but would just like to say to those of you expecting to visit 'Trent Bridge' in Nottingham. If you want a good local pub not more than a 5 minute walk from the ground, I recommend the Trent Navigation. It was refurbished about 12 months ago, and provides a fantastic atmosphere, with fantastic food.

As it's slightly off the beaten track (but not far from the stadium) it's probably a fair bit quieter than the local ones.

Please do check it out if you're in the local area. :)
